Overview

The RS rides as well as a Carrera on sports suspension; the steering is no heavier, and the car we drove featured air-con (a no-cost option) and sat-nav. Find an open road, though, and it comes alive: the steering is superb, the damping exceptional, and the engine just keeps pulling.

How reliable is the Porsche GT2 RS?

Porsche has worked hard to increase reliability and is now starting to build a reputation for itself as a highly dependable brand in today's luxury car market. While there have been multiple tweaks to the engine, the basic design is now the power behind the second generation of the 911, which has already proved reliable.

Drive wheels - Traction - Drivetrain: RWD

Other Features

3.8-liter Twin-Turbocharged Flat-Six Engine: The GT2 RS is powered by a highly advanced version of Porsche's iconic flat-six engine. With twin turbochargers, this engine delivers exceptional power and torque output.

700 Horsepower Output: The 3.8-liter engine in the GT2 RS produces a staggering 700 horsepower, making it one of the most powerful engines ever fitted in a production Porsche.

Rear-Wheel Drive: The engine's power is sent to the rear wheels through a sophisticated rear-wheel-drive system, ensuring maximum traction and performance.

Overboost Functionality: The engine features an overboost function that temporarily increases the boost pressure, providing even more power for short bursts of acceleration.

High-Performance Exhaust System: Porsche engineers have designed a high-performance exhaust system specifically for the GT2 RS, optimizing airflow and enhancing engine efficiency.

Why Should We Love This Car?

1. Exquisite Beauty: The Porsche GT2 RS is not just a high-performance machine; it's a work of art. Its sleek, aerodynamic design is sculpted to perfection, with every line and curve meticulously crafted for both aesthetic appeal and functional performance.

2. Luxurious Comfort: Despite its track-focused capabilities, the GT2 RS doesn't compromise on comfort. Step inside, and you'll find a meticulously crafted interior that blends luxury with performance. Premium materials, such as fine leather and carbon fiber, adorn every surface, while supportive seats provide comfort during long drives.
